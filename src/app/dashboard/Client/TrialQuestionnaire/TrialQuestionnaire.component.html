Teaser Questionnaire

Rate Yourself On A Scale From 1 To 4, With 1 Being The Most Likely And 4 Being The Least Likely. Go To Help For More Assistance.

This Is An Example Of How The Questionnaire Will Look:


I describe myself as:
1. I always think of everyone else and forget about myself
2. People must not touch my things
3. I love surprises and enjoy being spontaneous
4. I am reserved and self controlled
How do I interact with people:
5. I am sensitive to people's feelings and often take things personally
6. I get irritated easily with people
7. People's success stories motivate me
8. I have one or two special friends
